Research

Computer Vision and Image Understanding (CVIU) Lab was founded by Dr. Khoa Luu in Fall 2018 after he moved to University of Arkansas from Carnegie Mellon University, USA. CVIU Lab promotes advanced research in Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Biometrics, Machine Intelligence, and Quantum Machine Learning technologies.

There are five key research topics in CVIU Lab: Autonomous Driving & Perception, Quantum Machine Learning, Smart Health, Precision Agriculture, and Biometric Privacy Protection.
