Course Description

The objective of this class is to give students a hands-on introduction to the fundamentals of full-stack deep learning. A variety of deep learning techniques and applications will be discussed.

Prerequisites

This course requires familarity with deep learning, machine learning, and programming.

Programming Language: students can use any programming language, e.g. python, C/C++, Matlab, Java, for their assignments and projects.

Textbook

Relevant research papers will be introduced in each lecture. Students are highly recommended to read the textbooks and the assigned research papers.

Grading

Your final grade will be made up from:

  • Participation: 2%
  • Four assignments: 40%
  • Midterm Exam: 18%
  • Final Project: 40%

Late days: Students are allowed FIVE free late days in total. Any additional late days will each incur a 10% penalty.
